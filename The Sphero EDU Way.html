The Sphero EDU Way

Step 1: Download Sphero EDU and add it to Chrome and Open it

Step 2: Click on sign in and Click I am a Home user

Step 3: Click "Sign in with Google"

Step 4: Where it says "sign in", there is a blue colored text saying "Sphero EDU". Click on it and click on the email.

Step 5: Click on "Sign in" at the top right of the page

Step 6: Click on "Learn More" under the email adress box

Step 7: On the adress bar above, type in "*" and click on search on google

Step 8: Enjoy!
